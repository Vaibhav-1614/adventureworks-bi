# Business Questions This Dashboard Answers

## Executive

- Are revenue and order volume trending up or down month over month?
- How much of revenue is online vs offline?
- Are we on track against YTD expectations?

## Sales

- Which salespeople and territories are leading performance this month?
- Who is above or below quota?
- Where are coaching or resource changes needed?

## Product

- Which products and categories drive the most revenue and margin?
- Which products have poor margin or high return rates?
- Are discount patterns reducing profitability?

## Customer

- Which customer segments create the highest value?
- How many customers are new vs returning each month?
- Which customers are at risk or likely to churn?

## Anomaly Monitoring

- Which metrics show unusual spikes or drops versus historical behavior?
- Are anomalies concentrated in specific products, territories, or salespeople?
- What should leadership investigate before the monthly review?
